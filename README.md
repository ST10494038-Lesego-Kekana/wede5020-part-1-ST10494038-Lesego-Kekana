Our key features include handmade products like freshly baked muffins, scones, cookies and more. We have the online or rather digital ordering applications like whatsapp or filling in an online form which is another user friendly way that people enjoy using.Engagement with our community which includes youth mentorship programs and supplying our partnerships.you also get customer interactions, where customers will give you feedbacks regarding the service we offer or rather how our goods taste and what is it that can be improved.
